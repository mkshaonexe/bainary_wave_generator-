🎯 WHAT DO THE PRESETS DO?

🧘 MEDITATION (6 Hz)
   - Best for: Daily meditation practice
   - Reduces overthinking and mental chatter
   - Promotes deep relaxation

😴 SLEEP (5 Hz)  
   - Best for: Falling asleep or naps
   - Helps transition to deep sleep
   - Calms racing thoughts

💡 CREATIVITY (7 Hz)
   - Best for: Creative work or brainstorming
   - Enhances intuition and imagination
   - Light, focused relaxation

🌊 DEEP RELAX (4.5 Hz)
   - Best for: Deep meditation or stress relief
   - Maximum relaxation effect
   - Reduces anxiety
